Guide To Getting Free Cybersecurity Help

Whether You're An NGO, SME, Or Local Authority In Guinea, Getting Cybersecurity Support Is Simple And Completely Free. Follow These Four Guided Steps To Request Help And Receive Your Diagnostic Report.

1

Register Your Organization

Sign up in minutes by providing your organization's name, type (NGO, SME, local authority), and contact details. It’s quick and secure.

2

Request Cyber Help

Use our secure form to explain what you need checked — such as a website, emails, internal system, or network. You can upload documents or describe your concerns directly in the platform.
